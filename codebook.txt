The data in this dataset is a tidied data from an accelometer test

The data is gicing you the following information:

trainSubject: integer
1-30
The subject of the test, that tis the person subject to the test. Different persons are inentified with a number.

Activity: factor
The activity the subject was doing during the measurements. The variable has six factors:
LAYING 
SITTING 
STANDING 
WALKING 
WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS 
WALKING_UPSTAIRS

the rest 66 variables are the mean values of the calculated mean and standard deviation

mean(): Mean value
std(): Standard deviation
